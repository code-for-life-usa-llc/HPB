How Can You Help



There is something called defunding the police department. Defunding
the police means taking money away from funding the police department
and instead sending those funds to invest in the communities that are
struggling the most and where most of the policing occurs. The police
force should not be known as people who make a lot of arrest but people
who make the community feel safer.

Police academy training must go beyond firearms and defensive tactics.
Instead, it should emphasize communication skills and methods for
recognizing and de-escalating potentially violent situations. Some
academies do teach officers how to de-escalate confrontation, but not to
the extent necessary and not as often as they should after the officer is
sworn.
