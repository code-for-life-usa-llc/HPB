TEAM HPB

How do YOU feel about Police Brutality? There are countless accounts of police officers supposedly "protecting" people who live quaint lives, only to usurp their government-granted power. Your opinion on this matter is what we care about. We are Team HPB and we created a website designed specifically to bring to light all types of underreported police brutality cases, so that greater justice can be achieved. We have a personal connection to the subject, as some of our families have experienced Police Brutality.
That is why we're here. To inform you.
